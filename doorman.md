
Join the Union: In NYC, apply to join 32BJ SEIU, the largest property service workers union. Union buildings guarantee standardized prevailing wage rates, comprehensive family healthcare, and pensions, often pushing seasoned doormen's salaries well past $60,000 to $80,000 base.

Target Luxury Condominiums: High-end condos (rather than rental buildings) and white-glove co-ops feature affluent residents who dole out generous holiday bonuses. In luxury buildings, these tips can easily double your annual income.

Leverage Hotel Concierge/Doorman Roles: Transition to five-star hotels (e.g., The Plaza, The Carlyle). While base salaries vary, luxury hotels offer robust tipping cultures, union protections (like the New York Hotel Trades Council), and opportunities to advance into a Chef Concierge role.

Obtain Certifications: Differentiate yourself by taking hospitality courses. Earning a certification from the American Hotel & Lodging Educational Institute or completing specialized concierge training (such as through Les Clefs d'Or) makes you a top-tier candidate for the most lucrative white-glove positions.

Build an Elite Network: High-net-worth residents value discretion and white-glove service above all. 

Impress building managers and guests, as the most exclusive doorman openings are frequently filled through professional networking and word-of-mouth rather than public job boards.
